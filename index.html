Welcome to PikaPedia!

the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia

PikaPedia is an encyclopedia about Pokémon to which anyone can contribute. Since its launch in 2024, it has grown to become one of the largest Pokémon resources on the internet. You're welcome to browse for a while, if you wish, or make an account to start editing pages.
